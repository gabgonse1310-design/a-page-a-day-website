Delete your A Page a Day account

You can request permanent deletion of your account and associated data.

From the app

  1. Open A Page a Day.
  2. Go to Profile.
  3. Open Data & Privacy.
  4. Select Delete my account and confirm.

By email

Email gabgonse1310@gmail.com from the Google account connected to the app with the subject A Page a Day Account Deletion Request.

Data deleted

  • User profile and email stored by the app
  • Reading progress and completed lessons
  • XP, achievements, streaks and collection progress
  • Cloud preferences

We aim to complete verified deletion requests within 30 days.
