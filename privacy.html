Privacy Policy

Last updated: 20 July 2026

We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information.

Information we collect

  • Name and email address from your Google account
  • Unique user identifier
  • Reading progress, completed lessons and journeys
  • XP, achievements and reading streaks
  • Reminder preferences
  • Subscription status, when applicable

We do not intentionally collect precise location, contacts, messages, photographs or payment-card details.

How we use your information

  • Save and synchronize reading progress
  • Restore your account on another device
  • Track achievements and streaks
  • Provide optional reminders
  • Operate and improve the app

We do not sell personal information.

Third-party services

A Page a Day uses Google Sign-In, Firebase Authentication and Cloud Firestore. These services may process information under their own privacy policies.

Data security

Account information and progress are stored using Google Firebase services. We use reasonable measures to help protect data against unauthorized access, loss or misuse.

Data retention

We retain data while your account remains active or while needed to provide the app. When your account is deleted, associated personal data will be removed unless retention is required by law.

Your choices and rights

You may turn off reminders, sign out, request access to or deletion of your data, and request deletion of your account. See our Account Deletion page.

Children's privacy

A Page a Day is intended for users aged 16 and older.
